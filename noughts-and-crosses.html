Noughts and Crosses

© Just Fun N Games
How to play!
To play Noughts and Crosses (Tic-Tac-Toe), two players take turns marking an 'X' or 'O' in a 3x3 grid, aiming to get three of their symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, while blocking their opponent from doing the same; the first to achieve this wins, or it's a draw if the grid fills up.
Setup
Grid will be drawn: Nine squares.
Choose Symbols: Decide who plays 'X' (Crosses) and who plays 'O' (Noughts).
Decide Who Goes First: The player with 'X' usually goes first.
Gameplay
Players Take Turns: Players alternate placing their chosen symbol in any empty square on the grid.
Aim to Win: The goal is to get three of your symbols in a straight line (across, down, or diagonally).
Block Your Opponent: Simultaneously, watch for your opponent's potential lines and place your symbol to block them.
